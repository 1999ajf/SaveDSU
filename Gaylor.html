A hand slaps a mask over your face as you stumble outside. You turn to see Professor Gaylor lying in the grass a gas mask strapped to his face.

“I fucked up bad,” He says.

“My experiment worked but it got out and caused a power surge!”

He shoves a case into your hand that appears to be stuffed full of small vials with a luminescent blue liquid swirling inside.

“Give these to the professors, but be careful they seem to have gone crazy. You should stop by Sodexo if you can to get some energy. Stay safe, Chem nerd.”

Gaylor limps back toward the science center as you look around campus trying to decide which building to head to first.
